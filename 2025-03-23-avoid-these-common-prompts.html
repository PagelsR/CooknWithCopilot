Avoid These Common Prompts

Posted on March 23, 2025

Some prompt styles can lead to vague or irrelevant suggestions. Here’s what to avoid:

  • Prompts that are too generic, like "write some code."
  • Mixing multiple goals in one comment.
  • Forgetting to specify input/output expectations.
